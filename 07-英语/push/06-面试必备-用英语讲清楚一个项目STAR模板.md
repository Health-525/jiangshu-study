# 面试必备：用英语讲清楚一个项目（STAR + 技术词）

很多人英语不差，但一讲项目就散：细节太多、重点不清、结果说不出来。

最稳的办法是用 **STAR** 结构，把技术词放在该出现的位置。

---

## 1）STAR 模板（可直接套用）

### S — Situation（背景）
- What was the context?
- 例句：Our service handled high traffic during promotions.

### T — Task（目标）
- What did you need to achieve?
- 例句：My task was to reduce p99 latency and improve stability.

### A — Action（行动）
- What did you do?
- 常用词：**design / implement / refactor / migrate / optimize / monitor**
- 例句：I implemented caching, added observability, and optimized database queries.

### R — Result（结果）
- What was the measurable outcome?
- 常用词：**improved / reduced / increased / prevented / saved**
- 例句：We reduced p99 latency from 800ms to 300ms and lowered error rate by 40%.

---

## 2）项目描述的高频技术词（建议收藏）
- **architecture**：架构
- **trade-off**：权衡
- **bottleneck**：瓶颈
- **root cause**：根因
- **mitigation**：缓解措施（止血/降低影响）
- **observability**：可观测性（logs/metrics/tracing）
- **incident response**：事件响应

---

## 3）30 秒口播模板（英文）
> In this project, the main challenge was ____.
> My goal was to ____.
> I took three actions: ____, ____, and ____.
> As a result, we achieved ____ (numbers), and improved ____.
